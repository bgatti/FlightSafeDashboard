## Hours of Operation

- **Days:** Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday (closed Wednesday per some sources; other sources list Wed–Sun — call to confirm)
- **Season:** Year-round, weather permitting
- **Daylight operations only** — sunset flights possible if sufficient ground lighting for tow plane ops
- **Best flying:** 9 AM – 11:30 AM (smoothest); afternoon has better thermals but bumpier

## Flight Instruction

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Discovery Flight** | First glider experience — ground brief, aircraft familiarization, flight time at the controls ($245) |
| **Ground Instruction** | Aircraft tour, runway orientation, basic mechanics, rules & regulations |
| **Flight Instruction** | Stick-and-rudder fundamentals with certified glider instructors |
| **Soaring Techniques** | Advanced thermal and mountain wave soaring |
| **Spin Training / Upset Recovery** | Specialized endorsement training in Schweizer 2-32 |
| **BFR / Flight Review** | 1 hr ground + 1 hr flight for licensed pilots |

- **FAA examiner on staff** for checkrides
- Instruction leads to official glider pilot certification

## Policies

- **Cancellation:** No fee unless no-show
- **Payment:** Charged after the flight, not at booking
- **Weather:** No flights in rain; cancellations handled day-of or night before with free rescheduling
- **Photography:** Cameras/phones allowed; GoPro wing mounts available
- **Visitors:** Public welcome on the patio to observe; dogs allowed
- **Group/Private:** Available by phone inquiry
- **Gift Certificates:** Available for all flight packages (1 or 2 passengers)
